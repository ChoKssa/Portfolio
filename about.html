Who am I ?

Personal profile

My name is Alexandre, I am 19 years old and I am a student in computer science at EPITECH Paris.

What do I do ?

Thanks to EPITECH's pedagogy, I learn to be autonomous, to adapt according to the problems we encounter and to learn by ourselves. This pedagogy consists in learning through the practice of concrete projects. This school teaches us to code in C but conditions us to turn to languages and technologies that interest us personally.

What are my goals ?

I like to learn and try new things. My ultimate goal is to find the field that interests me the most and dedicate my life to it. But for now I'm discovering new things and investing my time in projects that I love (in parallel to my studies).
